Mashed Potatoes

Description

These flavor-packed garlic mashed potatoes are creamy, delicious, and impossible to resist.

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Combine potatoes, garlic, and 1 tablespoon of the salt in a medium saucepan with enough water to cover by 1 inch. Bring to a boil over high. Reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer until potatoes are fork-tender, 10 to 12 minutes.
  2. Drain potatoes and return to hot saucepan. Stir until dry, about 1 minute.
  3. Add butter, 1 piece at a time, stirring gently until melted before the next addition.
  4. Using a potato masher, mash in milk until smooth. Stir in 1 teaspoon salt and white pepper.
  5. Transfer potatoes to a serving bowl and top with more butter, if desired.
